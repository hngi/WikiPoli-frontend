Frequently Asked Questions



What is WikiPoli?

It is a special type of website designed to make contribution on everything politics easy. Many people are constantly improving Wikipoli, making thousands of edits every minute.

How can I contribute?

Making a contribution is so easy. simply click here and begin to share your wonderful contributions./p>

I am finding it difficult to log in, what should I do?

First, check your username and password. If they are correct, then one of two things may have happened. If you were sent an email, follow the instructions. If you did not receive an email, you may have provided an incorrect email address or the email may have been picked up by a spam filer. If you are sure the email address you provided is correct, try contacting an administrator.

What is the difference between a Super Administrator and an Administrator?

Super power!It is the super power bestowed on the super administrator to block users or administrators who default

What is the role of an Administrator?

Simple! An administrator plays the role of inviting or deleting a user. The admin can also delete posts completely from database, edit posts, publish posts and ensure it is visible for all.

How Do I Create a new topic or post a reply?

To post a new topic in a forum, click "Post". To post a reply to a topic, click "comment". You may need to register before you can post a message.

Can I comment on any post?

Of course! All you need to do is to go the comment section after every post and make a comment

I cannot rememeber my password?

Don’t panic! While your password cannot be retrieved, it can easily be reset. Visit the login page and click I forgot my password. Follow the instructions and you should be able to log in again shortly.
However, if you are not able to reset your password, contact a board administrator.

Why did I receive a warning?

There are set rules for this site. If you have broken a rule, you may be issued a warning. Please note that this is the administrator’s decision, and hng6 has nothing to do with the warnings on the given site. Contact the administrator if you are unsure about why you were issued a warning.

How can I report posts to an administrator?

If the administrator has allowed it, you should fill the "feedback" form.
